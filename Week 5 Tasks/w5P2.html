Melbourne

The Most Popular Landmarks in Melbourne are:

Flinders Street, the main train station located in the CBD. It was built in 1905.

Chadstone shopping centre. It is the biggest shopping centre in Melbourne

Federation Square. The main square located accross Flinders Street Station

Sydney

The Most Popular Landmarks in Sydney are:

The Sydney Opera House. It was built in 1959.

Sydney Harbour Bridge. It was built in 1932 and it carries rail, vehicular, bicycle and pedestrian traffic

Bondi Beach. It is one of the most popular beaches in Australia with thousands of tourists every year.

Canberra

The Most Popular Landmarks in Canberra are:

Parliament House. It was built in 1988 and it is the meeting place of the Australian Parliament

The Australian War Memorial is Australia's national memorial to the members of its armed forces

The National Gallery of Australia. It was established in 1953.
